I hope this email finds you well. I'd just like to take this opportunity to give a few more details about the implementation of our COVID-19 safety policy, and to point out a few other miscellaneous things relating to COVID safety at SUNIA.

Rapid Tests

Firstly, as outlined in our COVID-19 safety plan, we will have government-issued rapid tests at the bus. We will be requiring a negative test result from all students before they board. If any student tests positive, they will unfortunately not be permitted to attend, and a full refund will be issued.

Booster Doses

To minimize the odds that you test positive before SUNIA, we strongly encourage you to get a booster dose ASAP if you're eligible. If you're under 18 and haven't gotten your first booster, we encourage you to do so ASAP.

Proof of Vaccination

Also, we will be requiring proof of vaccination at the bus, by means of the Alberta-issued COVID-19 vaccine record (for students from Alberta). You can access your record here by entering your Alberta Personal Health Number, date of birth, and month of vaccination - once you have done so, please bring it with you to the bus (or to the Goldeye Centre directly, if you're not taking the bus). If you are from Alberta but do not have a Personal Health Number, you can get your record by calling 811. If you are not from Alberta, please follow your province's procedures for acquiring proof of vaccination.

All of us on staff look forward to a fun and safe SUNIA this August!
