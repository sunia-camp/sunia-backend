Refund Policy

In the case that you cannot attend SUNIA for any reason, and you wish to cancel your registration, you must do so in the next three days. After these three days, and prior to July 1st 2023, we will charge a $100 cancellation fee. If you have already paid, this means we will refund you all but $100 of your payment. Cancellations received on or after July 1st will not receive a refund. In the case of extenuating circumstances (such as COVID-19 infection or outbreak), please contact me and we can discuss the possibility of a full refund, or other options.

Last thing: please do not hesitate to reach out to me at registrar@sunia.ca with any questions or concerns you may have. Our staff looks forward to welcoming you to SUNIA this August!
