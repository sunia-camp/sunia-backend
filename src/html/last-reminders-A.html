Vaccination Records

As per my previous emails and our COVID-19 safety plan, we will be requiring proof of vaccination at the bus, by means of the Alberta-issued COVID-19 vaccine record (for students from Alberta). Please make sure you've downloaded your vaccination record from here before you head out to SUNIA. If you are from Alberta but do not have a Personal Health Number, please call 811 to get your record. If you are from outside of Alberta, please follow your own province's procedures for acquiring proof of vaccination and bring that to the bus.
